  • RWBC-OIL Activities at ISC19

AIST researchers, TokyoTech researchers and students who belong to RWBC-OIL attend ISC19 to provide presentations about our latest researches. We also have a booth in the exhibition floor to present our two super computing systems , ABCI and TSUBAME3.0, and recent research achievements conducted on them. Please visit our booth to have discussion!

ISC19 Booth Exhibition

RWBC-OIL mission is to advance the high-end processing and applications of big data in the real world, by utilizing high-performance computing as well as AI-based analysis technologies on world-leading computing infrastructures. Collaborating with top-tier global research institutions as well as industries, this effort focuses on areas such as video recognition, drug design, and social simulations.

Highlights at ISC 2019 is two super computing systems: AI Bridging Cloud Infrastructure (ABCI) of AIST and TSUBAME3.0 of TokyoTech. ABCI is an open innovation platform with world-class computing resources of 550 Pflops (HP) / 37 Pflops (DP) for AI research and development. It is ranked #7 in Top500 and #4 in Green500 lists as of November 2018. TSUBAME3.0 is a successor of TSUBAME which is a pionioor of GPU-base supercomputer. It is ranked #22 in Top500 and #5 in Green500 lists as of November 2018. Further information about ABCI is available from ABCI web site and information about TSUBAME3.0 is available from TSUBAME3.0 site.
